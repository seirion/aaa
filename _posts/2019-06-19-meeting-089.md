### Trees as a Service

[문제 링크](hhttps://www.facebook.com/hackercup/problem/330920680938986/)

#### Constraints
* 1 ≤ T ≤ 100 
* 2 ≤ N ≤ 60 
* 1 ≤ M ≤ 120 
* 1 ≤ Xi, Yi, Zi ≤ N 
* Xi ≠ Yi
